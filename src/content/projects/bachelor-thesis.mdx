The thesis explores why magnetic monopoles are theoretically compelling: the duality of Maxwell's equations, Dirac's quantization condition, gauge potentials, and the topological consequences of introducing magnetic charge.

The final part considers experimental searches, from superconducting induction devices to highly ionizing signatures and collider experiments.
